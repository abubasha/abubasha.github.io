Brookefields is a shopping mall located on Brookebond Road (Krishnasamy Road) in Coimbatore, India. It was opened in 2009. The mall has outlets from major clothing and apparel brands and a six screen multiplex cinema, along with a food court serving multi-cuisine dishes.

Fun Republic Mallclose

Fun Republic Mall is a shopping mall in Peelamedu, Coimbatore, India. This shopping mall was inaugurated on 19 August 2012 and Managed by E-city Ventures which is part of the Essel Group. It is located on the arterial Avinashi Road on a 3.5 acre site.The mall has 6 levels with a total area of 5.25 lakh sq.ft. Shoppers Stop is its anchor store and occupies over 79,000 sq. ft. The mall also features a five screen multiplex operated by Fun Cinemas with a capacity of 1,119 seats and a McDonalds restaurant spread over 3,470 sq. ft on two floors in addition to its food court.

Prozone Mall is a horizontal design on the lines of international shopping centers. It has been envisioned as a dynamic new retail-led mixed-use destination. The genesis of the concept is the creation of multi-use development that assimilates venues for retail, entertainment and leisure. Prozone Mall, strategically located on the Sathyamangalam road brings the international standard hub housing over 100 National & International Brands across categories like apparel, footwear, jewellery, accessories, F&B, entertainment, and much more. Being Coimbatores first integrated township, with luxuriously designed residential spaces & A Shopping mall, it is set to be the talk of town and the apple of every customers eye
